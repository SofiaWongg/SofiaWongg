POUR COLLECTION

This collection involves chaotic pour paint created through the use of bold colors and bold paint movements. A majority of these were created through a joint project with Corbin Bumeter in 2019.

“Wack Abstract” (8.5 x 12 – Acrylic pour paint on canvas – 2020) A very vibrant and wild piece. Chaotic pour paint created through the use of bold colors and bold paint movements.

“Black and White” (8.5 x 12 – Acrylic pour paint on canvases – joint project with Corbin Bumeter – 2019) An elegant piece created with just two colors.
